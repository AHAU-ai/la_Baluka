# Disputes & corrections

This corpus makes claims about a living people's sacred inheritance. The people
best placed to find an error are not the builders. This is the channel that turns
"someone knows this is wrong and we can't hear them" into a captured signal.

## How to raise a dispute
Open an issue titled `DISPUTE: <arc> beat <n>` (or email the cooperative at the
address on labaluka.net) with: the arc and beat, what is contested (faithfulness
of the script, the interpretation, orthography, the right to publish at all), and
your standing if you wish to give it.

## What happens
1. The corpus is placed on **hold** (`npm run hold "dispute: <ref>"`) if the
   dispute alleges harm or misrepresentation — fail-safe first, discuss second.
2. The lineage accountability holder and any named attestor review.
3. Outcome is recorded: correction (re-seal + canary review), **retraction**
   (`npm run retract`), or a reasoned decline, logged in the ledger and here.

No dispute is closed by the machine. A green checkmark never overrides a person.
